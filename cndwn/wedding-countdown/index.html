Frequently asked questions

Should I use a countdown or a progress bar for my engagement?

A countdown shows the days remaining until the wedding, while a progress bar shows how far along you are from your engagement date to the wedding day — many couples like using a progress bar to visualize the whole planning journey.

Can I share my wedding countdown with my partner or wedding party?

Yes. CNDWN lets you share a countdown so your partner, bridesmaids, groomsmen or family can follow the days until the big day too.

Can I also count down to my honeymoon?

Yes. Create a second countdown for your honeymoon departure date with its own color, separate from your wedding countdown.
