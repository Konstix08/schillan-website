Set up your trip countdown

A trip is more fun when you can watch the days tick away. Here's how to set it up in CNDWN.

  • ✓
    Add your departure date Type it in manually, or import it straight from Calendar if your flight or booking is already there.
  • ✓
    Give it a color and emoji A palm tree for the beach, a mountain for a ski trip — make it instantly recognizable in your list.
  • ✓
    Pin it as a widget Add it to your Home Screen or Lock Screen so the days until departure are always in view.
  • ✓
    Share it with your travel companions Everyone coming along can follow the same countdown right up to takeoff.

Frequently asked questions

Can I track more than one trip at a time?

Yes. CNDWN has no limit on the number of countdowns, so you can track several upcoming trips at once, each with its own color and widget.

Should I use a countdown or a progress bar for a multi-day trip?

Use a countdown for the run-up to departure, then switch to a progress bar once you're traveling to see how much of the trip is left, from your start date to your return date.

Can I share my trip countdown with friends or family?

Yes. CNDWN lets you share a countdown so your travel companions or family can follow the days until departure too.
