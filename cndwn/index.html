Frequently asked questions

Everything you might want to know before you download.

What is CNDWN?

CNDWN is a countdown app for Apple devices. It lets you count down to birthdays, holidays, trips, deadlines and any other important date, and shows the time remaining right on your Home Screen and Lock Screen with widgets. It runs on iPhone, iPad, Mac, Apple Watch and Apple TV.

Which devices does CNDWN work on?

CNDWN works on iPhone, iPad, Mac, Apple Watch and Apple TV. Your countdowns sync automatically across all of your Apple devices through your own iCloud account.

Is CNDWN private? Does it collect my data?

Yes, CNDWN is privacy-first. Your countdowns are stored locally on your device and, if you enable sync, in your own private iCloud account. CNDWN has no ads, no third-party trackers, and does not sell your data. The developer has no access to your countdowns. Read the full Privacy Policy.

Does CNDWN have Home Screen and Lock Screen widgets?

Yes. CNDWN includes Home Screen and Lock Screen widgets so you can see the days, hours and minutes remaining until your most important events without opening the app.

Can I create a countdown from a calendar event?

Yes. With your permission, CNDWN can read events from your calendar so you can turn any event into a countdown in a couple of taps. Calendar data is processed on your device and is never sent to the developer.

How much does CNDWN cost?

CNDWN is available on the Apple App Store. You can download it and start creating countdowns for free.
