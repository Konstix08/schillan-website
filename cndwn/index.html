Frequently asked questions

Everything you might want to know before you download.

What is CNDWN?

CNDWN is a countdown and countup app for Apple devices. It lets you count down to birthdays, holidays, trips and deadlines — or count up since a past moment — and shows the time remaining right on your Home Screen, Lock Screen and StandBy with widgets, Live Activities and progress bars. It runs on iPhone, iPad, Mac and Apple Watch.

Is CNDWN really free?

Yes. CNDWN is the first countdown app that gives you every feature for free — countdowns, countups, progress bars, widgets, Live Activities, iCloud sync, custom colors and more. There are no ads and no trackers.

Which devices does CNDWN work on?

CNDWN works on iPhone, iPad, Mac and Apple Watch and requires iOS 18.0 or later. Your countdowns sync automatically across all of your Apple devices through your own iCloud account, and Handoff lets you pick up where you left off.

What's the difference between a countdown, a countup and a progress bar?

A countdown shows the time remaining until a future date. A countup shows how much time has passed since a date in the past. A progress bar shows how far along you are between a start and an end date — great for projects, semesters or milestones.

Does CNDWN have widgets and Live Activities?

Yes. CNDWN includes Home Screen, Lock Screen and StandBy widgets, plus Live Activities that show your countdown on the Lock Screen and in the Dynamic Island — so you can see the time remaining without opening the app.

Can I create a countdown from my calendar or reminders?

Yes. With your permission, CNDWN can read events from Calendar and Reminders so you can turn any event into a countdown in a couple of taps. This data is processed on your device and is never sent to the developer.

What occasions can I use CNDWN for?

Anything with a date attached: birthdays, weddings, anniversaries, exams, graduations, vacations, pregnancies, product launches, concerts, sobriety milestones, moving day or a long-distance relationship reunion. If it has a date, CNDWN can count down — or up — to it.

Is there a good countdown app for vacations or trips?

Yes — CNDWN is a popular choice for vacation countdowns. Add your trip's departure date, give it a custom color and emoji, and pin it as a widget to your Home Screen or Lock Screen so you can watch the days tick down until you leave.

Can I use CNDWN to count down to an exam or deadline?

Yes. Import the date directly from Calendar or Reminders, or add it manually, then use a progress bar to see how much study or work time is left, or a countdown to see exactly how many days, hours and minutes remain.

Is CNDWN private? Does it collect my data?

Yes, CNDWN is privacy-first. Your countdowns are stored on your device and, if you enable sync, in your own private iCloud account. CNDWN has no ads, no third-party trackers, and does not sell your data. The developer has no access to your countdowns. Read the full Privacy Policy.
