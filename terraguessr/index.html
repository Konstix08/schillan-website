How a round works

You're there. Where is "there"?

No two rounds feel the same — anywhere on Earth is fair game.

1

Get dropped in

Each round places you at a real, unlabeled spot using Apple's Look Around — no map, no hints, just the view around you.

2

Read the clues

Look around for road signs, architecture, vegetation and anything else that gives away the region — or the exact city.

3

Drop your pin

Place your guess on the map before the clock runs out. The closer you are, the more points — or damage — you score.

One shot a day. Make it count.

Five locations, sixty seconds each, one attempt per day. Your total distance ranks you on the daily leaderboard — and every day you play keeps your streak alive.

Questions

Frequently asked questions

Everything you might want to know before launch.

What is TerraGuessr?

TerraGuessr is a geography guessing game for iPhone. Each round drops you into a real place using Apple's Look Around, and you explore your surroundings for clues before placing a pin on the map to guess where you are. The closer your guess, the higher you score.

How does scoring work in a Duel?

In a 1v1 Duel, both players see the same location at the same time. The closer your guess is to the real spot, the more damage you deal to your opponent. The first player to deal 6,000 damage wins the duel.

What is the Daily Challenge?

Five locations, sixty seconds each, once per day. Everyone plays the same rounds, so you can compare your total distance against friends and the global leaderboard — and build a daily streak.

Can I play with friends?

Yes. Add friends to see who's online, start a private Party and share a code so they can jump straight in, or join a friend's Party directly from your friends list. Duels and Squads also support matchmaking if you'd rather play against anyone.

Do I need Game Center to play?

Signing in with Game Center is recommended — it saves your progress, friends and stats and syncs them across your Apple devices.

What devices does TerraGuessr support?

TerraGuessr is built for iPhone and requires iOS 18 or later.

When is TerraGuessr coming out?

TerraGuessr is currently in development. There's no release date yet — check back here, or email us for early access, and we'll let you know as soon as it's ready.
